Location Clues

Here are the clues! Can you find each of these places on campus?

Look out for the Trail to the Future posterboards. You must find the location on campus and scan the QR. There you will see photos, or have a recording to listen to. You will be asked to answer a question based on these.

Once you have all visited all seven locations, you can input them in the form below. You will then be emailed the final location, where you can pick up your prize!
