About

Memories in Place was developed as part of a PhD being undertaken at Open Lab at the School of Computing, Newcastle University. The aim of this project is to explore digital tools for exploring local heritage and see how these can be used to support community heritage projects and through this to encourage community participation in local areas.

For Newcastle University's Experience Week the project has been re-purposed to support a scavenger hunt around the University enabling user to find and listen to audio clips reflecting on the University's past, present and future. Users are also invited to leave their own thoughts about the University which will be passed on to the Experience Week team and may be used in future activities by them.

If you have any questions or concerns and would like to get in touch with us please use the contact form below.
