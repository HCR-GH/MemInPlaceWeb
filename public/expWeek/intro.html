Trail to the Future

Welcome to the Trail to the Future Scavenger Hunt

As part of Experience Week 11-15 March, we are taking you on a tour around campus. However, you have to solve clues to do it. Follow the clues on this page to find seven locations across campus.

Once you find the Qr codes you can scan it to listen to more about the location and see photos from it. You will be asked a question based on these.

When you have visited all seven locations and submitted the answers to the questions we ask you about them, we will email you a final location where you can collect your prize!
