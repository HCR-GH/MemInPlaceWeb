Record your own memory

Inspired by what you've heard to share your own memories of this place? Then use the recording function below to record and share your memory.

Memories shared here may be studied and used anonymously in research outputs such as papers and presentations and as materials in further research activities connected to this project. They may also be made available to listen to publicly. If you do not wish your memory to be used in these ways please do not submit it. For more information please see the about page. If you've any questions or concerns and wish to get in touch please contact us here.

How to record

Press the record button to start your recording and the stop button when you are done. You can listen back to your recording in the audio player below.

If you'd like to re-do your recording press record again and your new recording will replace your previous one. Don't forget to allow microphone permissions if prompted.

Submit Your Recording

Use the form below to submit your recording to the project. If you want to leave your recording anonymously leave all the boxes blank and just tap upload.

Upload

Your recording has been submitted - if you'd like to download a copy to your device click the download button.
