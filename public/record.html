Record your own memory

Press the record button to start your recording and the stop button when you are done. You can listen back to your recording in the audio player below.

If you'd like to re-do your recording press record again and your new recording will replace your previous one. Don't forget to allow microphone permissions if prompted.

Submit Your Recording

Enter the name you would like to accompany your recording in the 'Display Name' box - leave this blank if you do not want a name published alongside your recording.

To leave a recording completely anonymously leave all the boxes blank.

Submitted recordings may be made available for others to listen to through this site and/or by Remembering the Past. They may also be used as part of a research project at Newcastle University considering digital tools for exploring local history. By submitting a recording you agree to it being used in these ways.

Save

Your recording has been submitted - if you'd like to download a copy to your device click the download button.
